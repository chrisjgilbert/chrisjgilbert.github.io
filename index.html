ABOUT

Nicola provides an individual service, combining strategic brand development with stellar contacts, an established reputation and a proven track record. Analyzing her clients’ business and commercial needs, she produces a bespoke strategy, from the sales approach to introducing internal structures, offering design and merchandising feedback, and conceptualizing creative marketing ideas from pop ups to collaborations.

Brand positioning is central to every decision, ensuring maximum growth and brand longevity whilst creating expansion opportunities and volume growth.

Over the past nine years, Nicola has worked in both the UK and USA, building up an impressive network of global industry contacts with key department stores, online retailers and specaility boutiques.
